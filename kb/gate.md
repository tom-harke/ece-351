SV supports, as modules
 - logic:   and, or, xor, nand, nor, xnor
 - buffers: buf, not
 - tri-state: bufif1, notif1, bufif0, notif0
 - etc: pulldown, pullup, nmos, pmos, cmos (?)

etc
 - gate name is optional, but helpful
 - gates can have more than 2 inputs
     and bi(out2,a,b);
     and tri(out3,a,b,c);
